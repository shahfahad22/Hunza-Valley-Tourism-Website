Hunza Serena Inn

Location: Karimabad, Central Hunza

A peaceful getaway with panoramic views of the Hunza Valley, Hunza Serena Inn offers a beautiful blend of comfort and tradition. The hotel is known for its eco-friendly practices and its proximity to historic sites like Baltit Fort and Altit Fort.

  • 🛏️ Spacious garden-facing rooms
  • 🍽️ Local and international cuisine
  • 🌐 Free Wi-Fi
  • 🚗 Guided Tours & Transport Services
  • 🏞️ Walking distance from Baltit Fort

Serena Hunza Hotel

Location: Hunza Valley

Serena Hunza Hotel is a luxurious stay in the heart of Hunza Valley, offering stunning views of the majestic mountains and serene environment. It combines modern comforts with traditional architectural beauty, making it a favorite for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.

  • 🛏️ Elegant and spacious rooms with valley views
  • 🍽️ Gourmet dining with local and international cuisines
  • 🌐 High-speed Wi-Fi throughout the hotel
  • 🚗 Airport transfers and guided tours available
  • 🏞️ Close proximity to key attractions like Rakaposhi viewpoint

Eagle's Nest Hotel

Location: Duikar, Hunza Valley

Perched high in the mountains, Eagle's Nest Hotel offers one of the most stunning panoramic views in Hunza. Perfect for witnessing breathtaking sunrises and sunsets, this hotel is a haven for nature lovers and photographers.

  • 🛏️ Rooms with balconies and mountain views
  • 🍽️ Traditional Hunzai and continental cuisine
  • 🔥 Outdoor bonfire area under the stars
  • 🌄 Famous sunrise & sunset viewpoints nearby
  • 🥾 Guided treks and nature walks

Hunza Darbar Hotel

Location: Aliabad, Hunza Valley

Hunza Darbar Hotel combines traditional Hunzai charm with modern amenities in the lively town of Aliabad. With beautiful mountain views and easy access to local markets and cultural landmarks, it’s an excellent choice for tourists who want to stay close to both nature and local life.

  • 🛏️ Comfortable rooms with scenic balconies
  • 🍽️ On-site restaurant offering local and international dishes
  • 💼 Conference & event facilities
  • 🌐 Free Wi-Fi and 24/7 service
  • 🛍️ Close to shops, markets, and cultural sites
