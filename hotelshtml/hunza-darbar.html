Hunza Darbar Hotel

Aliabad, Hunza Valley

Located in the vibrant town of Aliabad, Hunza Darbar Hotel is a blend of traditional architecture and modern comfort. With easy access to major attractions and markets, it’s perfect for travelers who want to experience local culture along with stunning views of the valley and mountains.

Facilities & Highlights

  • Comfortable rooms with private balconies
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant with local specialties
  • Conference halls and event facilities
  • Free Wi-Fi and room service
  • Located near local shops and cultural spots
