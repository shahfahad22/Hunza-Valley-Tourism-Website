Eagle's Nest Hotel

Duikar, Hunza Valley

Perched high above the Hunza Valley in the scenic village of Duikar, Eagle's Nest Hotel offers unmatched panoramic views of the surrounding snow-capped peaks, including Rakaposhi, Ultar, Ladyfinger, and more. The hotel is an ideal destination for those seeking peace, natural beauty, and the most breathtaking sunrises and sunsets in the region.

Facilities & Highlights

  • Rooms with private balconies and direct mountain views
  • Traditional Hunzai meals and continental cuisine
  • Outdoor bonfire and stargazing area
  • Walking distance to sunrise and sunset viewpoints
  • Guided treks, nature walks, and local cultural tours
